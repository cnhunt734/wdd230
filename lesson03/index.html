Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to current and potential business owners in the town of Dinosaur, Colorado. The main industry in Dinosaur is tourism, attracted by the various national monuments and outdoors activities surrounding the town, as well as other recreational retailers typical to the state of Colorado.
