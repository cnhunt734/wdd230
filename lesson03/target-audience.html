Target Audience

The target audience for the Dinosaur Chamber of Commerce website is business owners in town of Dinosaur, CO. This mostly includes small businesses and tourist attractions as the many available outdoor activities attract people from all over. Part of the target audience also will include tour guides, and rangers for the various trails and surrounding national monuments.

Personas

Sharon

  • Sharon is a small business owner who sells hand-carved dinosaur trinkets
  • Sharon is likely to use this site to check on calendar events, weather conditions, and other affairs that will indicate a high influx of visitors and potential clients for her to prepare for.

Jerry

  • Jerry is a park ranger who works for the National Monument Service.
  • Jerry is frequently assigned to work in Dinosaur and will visit the chamber of commerce site to gain insight on local events, holidays that will bring traffic, and weather conditions.

Listed Scenarios

  • To check for calendar events that would bring potential tourism
  • To check the weather and trail closures nearby
  • To check for seasonal jobs during busy periods
  • To get information on schools, parks, gyms, housing and other long-term stay considerations
  • To search a local business directory for specific needs
