Accomodations

Each of our hotels has a separate suite for full-time temple missionaries. Occupants will have added security and reduced distractions with a private elevator that takes them directly to their suite. The missionary suite also contains a kitchen where tenants can cook together, as well as a private fitness center for conducting morning workouts and P-day recreation. Each room has a bedroom and study area to ensure all mission needs are met.
