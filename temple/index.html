Temple of the Week

Mesa, AZ Temple

About

The Mesa Arizona Temple was the first temple built in Arizona and was announced in the year 1919. The vast temple grounds boast a beautiful display of God's many botanical creations, from fruit trees to cactus gardens, to palm tree walkways. Some unique features about the Mesa Temple is that it hosts the most significant annual Easter pageant. It is also one of seven temples that does not have a statue of the angel Moroni.
